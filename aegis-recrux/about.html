Aegis Recrux

A mod pack created for the Aegis Gaming PvE Server.

Battle the harsh environment, survive the onslaught of undead hordes and with a little help from your friends, build technological wonders to help you survive the day.

Aegis Recrux is a well balanced technological oriented modpack, with a focus on survival instincts and a lust for adventure. No place is safe, unless you make it so.
